Resume should predominantly contain short bullet points rather than paragraphs. If a recruiter was to pick up your resume right now, he should be able to skim through it in 30 seconds and your resume needs to be optimized for it. Short, succinct bullet points are key to your resume's readability. Blobs of text, that is bullets or paragraphs that are three lines or longer, are likely to not be read. It is recommended that you should list between 10 – 20 bullet points in your resume. Less than 10 might mean that you do not have enough relevant experience and more than 20 means that you have included non – relevant accomplishments in your resume which may be overshadowing your actual achievements. Aim for 4 – 6 bullet points per experience.
