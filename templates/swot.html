Fill In the Details


Use this template to create your personal Wheel of Life. Fill in the different fields with scores between 0 to 10 . Consider the questions mentioned in the sections while scoring your self, you can ask yourself more questions while doing the scoring. Your wheel of life will be generated automatically. The output may help you to set (life) goals. Reach out to our coaches in case you need further help.

CAREER
  • 1) How satisfied are you with your job, is it the job that you had imagined or would you rather pursue another career?

  • 2) Does the job bring you happiness and satisfaction?

  • 3) Are your earnings adequate for a comfortable living?


FINANCE
  • 1) Are all incomes enough to meet all basic needs?

  • 2) Are you dependent on loans?

  • 3) Do you worry about money regularly?

  • 4) Are you able to save or enjoy leisure activities?

  • 5) Is your happiness dependant on how much money you have?


CAREER GROWTH
  • 1) Do you have enough time for self development?

  • 2) Are you open to new experiences and eager to learn?

  • 3) Do you get enough opportunities to learn?

  • 4) How satisfied are you with your personal growth?


FAMILY AND FRIENDS
  • 1) Do you have many people such as friends or family who are supportive, good listeners and trustworthy?

  • 2) How is your social life?

  • 3) Do you have a good work-life balance?

  • 4) Do you do anything fun during your week?

  • 5) Do you feel isolated, lonely or find you have too many people around?

  • 6) What could you change for the better about relationships with family members?

  • 7) Would you like to be closer to the ones you love?


HEALTH
  • 1) How physically and mentally healthy are you and how do you feel?

  • 2) Are you satisfied with your appearance and weight?

  • 3) Do you have any physical discomforts or health concerns?

  • 4) To what extent are you engaged in sports?

  • 5) Is your diet balanced or do you regularly eat junk food?


LOVE AND RELATIONSHIPS
  • 1) Have you found happiness in love?

  • 2) Do you have a new or a committed partner on whom you can build a relationship?


FUN AND RECREATION
  • 1) Are you enjoying life?

  • 2) Do you practice hobbies or sports?

  • 3) How do you spend your free time?


SOCIETY
  • 1) Do you help others?

  • 2) Do you volunteer your time, money or resources in any way?

  • 3) To what extent are you active at (sports) clubs, in the neighbourhood or taking care of family members?
